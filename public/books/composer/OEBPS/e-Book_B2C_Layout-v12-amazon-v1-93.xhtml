The chords of minor keys

Remember from Day Four how major keys have certain chords that belong to them? Well, it’s the same with minor keys. For each note of the minor scale there is a corresponding chord:

R T S T T S T T

im iio III ivm vm VI VII im

Now take a look at this, because major and relative minor keys are related, the chords that belong to them are the same. You can see this clearly when you lay out both the major and relative minor scales, with the chords that belong to them below:

C Dm Em F G Am Bo C

Am Bo C Dm Em F G Am
