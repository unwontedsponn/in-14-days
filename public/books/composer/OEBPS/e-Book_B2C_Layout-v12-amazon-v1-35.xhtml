33 – Slave To The Rhythm – Day 2 – Understanding simple rhythm

Improvise for one minute, putting together your rhythmic phrase and the root note you chose on Day One. Here’s some ideas to get things rolling.

– Play hands together and apart.

– Play with one hand in the centre of the piano, the other in the higher/lower register (remember that word?)

– Play loudly with one hand, while the other plays softly (remember the word dynamics?)

– Try expressing different emotions.

– Try at 60bpm. Then try at 120bpm.

Now choose an excerpt from your improvisation to focus on. Try to make your root notes flow from one to the other. Think smooth. Repeat your excerpt until it becomes effortless.
