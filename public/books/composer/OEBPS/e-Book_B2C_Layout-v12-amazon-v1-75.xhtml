73 – He’s Got Two Left Hands – Day 7 – Left hand chord inversions

So far you’ve been playing your LH chords in what is known as root position. This simply means that the root note is the lowest note of the chord (R).

C major chord in root position:

C E G

R

The root note is also sometimes called the 1 of the chord:

C E G

1

E and G are called the 3 and the 5:

C E G

1 3 5
