Repeat symbol

Repeat symbols indicate a musical phrase that should be repeated by the player/s.

Relative minor

A minor key that shares the same notes as its relative major key.

Rhythm

Different combinations of long and short sounds and spaces. Rhythm is laid over a steady pulse/beat.

Root position

The notes of a chord, played in a specific shape where the lowest note is the root.

Scale

A group of notes played in order, ascending or descending.

Semitone

Two notes right next to each other, the smallest possible distance apart in music e.g. C and C# are a semitone apart.

Sharp / #

A note that is raised by one semitone, to become the sharp form of that note.
