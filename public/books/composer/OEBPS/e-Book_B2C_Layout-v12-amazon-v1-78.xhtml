Time to choose: Select a rhythm for your new LH chord inversions e.g. quarter notes.

Keeping a steady 60bpm pulse, play your LH chords as your chosen rhythm and remember that your chord changes will still always fall on beat one of each bar no matter what rhythm you are using.
