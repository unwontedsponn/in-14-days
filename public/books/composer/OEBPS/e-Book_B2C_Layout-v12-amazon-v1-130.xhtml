128 – Where You Lead – Day 14 – Creating your Lead Sheet

After middle C the notes ascend up the white keys, all the way up to the A an octave above. The curly symbol at the very start of the excerpt is called a treble clef. It’s this clef that assigns the five horizontal lines their notes (EGBDF). We call these five horizontal lines the stave.

There’s a handy way to remember what the spaces between the lines mean too, ‘FACE’:
